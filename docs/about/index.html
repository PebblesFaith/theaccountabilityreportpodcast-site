About

A public-interest investigative and educational project

The Accountability Report Podcast is hosted by Sarai Hannah Ajai. It examines documented concerns and accountability questions affecting consumers, small operators, and the public record.

Hosted by Sarai Hannah Ajai

Sarai Hannah Ajai is the host and founder of The Accountability Report Podcast, a public-interest investigative podcast focused on fraud awareness, identity theft documentation, digital misuse, consumer protection concerns, and accountability reporting. The podcast is designed to review documented concerns, public records where available, consumer protection pathways, and practical steps for preserving evidence.

Editorial mission

The podcast reviews documented concerns, public records where available, consumer protection processes, and practical steps for preserving evidence and reporting suspected fraud. Coverage is framed carefully and factually, with attention to what is reported versus what has been confirmed.

Sources and selection

Each episode draws on publicly available records, listener-submitted documentation, published agency guidance, and editorial review. Stories are selected for their broader relevance to identity protection, digital account security, intellectual property integrity, and consumer rights.

Scope and limitations

The podcast does not provide legal, financial, medical, or cybersecurity professional advice. Allegations referenced on the show are discussed as reported concerns unless confirmed by official findings.
